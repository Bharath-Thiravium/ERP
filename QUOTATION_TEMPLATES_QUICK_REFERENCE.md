## Choose Your Template

### 🎯 Clean & Simple Template (AS)
**"Clean 3-column header with logo panel, essential fields, single signature. Best for everyday use."**

```
╔════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║ 📋 PERFECT FOR:                               ║
║ • Daily quotations                             ║
║ • Small to medium businesses                   ║
║ • Standard product/service sales               ║
║ • Quick internal quotations                    ║
║ • Straightforward pricing                      ║
```

**Key Features:**
- Logo panel on left (15% width)
- Company details in center (55% width)
- Document title on right (30% width)
- Bill To / Ship To addresses
- Simple, clean black borders
- Single signature line
- Single-page format (37KB PDF)

**Key Features:**
- Teal gradient header (#0f766e to #0d5f5b)
- Compliance fields row:
  - Place of Supply (from customer state)
  - Reverse Charge indicator (Yes/No)
  - GST Type (IGST/CGST/SGST/Reverse)
- Prominent Amount in Words display
- Payment Terms table (4 structured rows):
  - Payment Mode
  - Due Date
  - Currency
  - Late Payment Terms
- Dual signatures:
  - "Authorized By" (Company)
  - "Customer Acceptance" (Customer)
- Professional white/teal color scheme
- Single-page format (19KB PDF - most compact)

## Template Selection Per Business Type

### Small Business (1-50 employees)
**Recommended**: Clean & Simple (AS)
- Simple, no-frills quotations
- Fast generation
- Professional appearance
- Single signature authority

### Growing Business (50-500 employees)
**Recommended**: Professional (BKGE)
- Client-facing proposals
- GST compliance tracking
- Dual approvals (Company + Customer)
- Structured payment terms
- Professional appearance with color

### Enterprise (500+ employees)
**Recommended**: Detailed Terms (TC)
- Complex contracts
- Multi-level approval
- Comprehensive compliance
- Bank details and declarations
- Premium presentation

## Technical Details

### PDF File Sizes
- **Clean & Simple**: 37KB (typical)
- **Professional**: 19KB (most compact)
- **Detailed Terms**: 44KB (comprehensive)

- All templates: Single page A4
- All templates: Print-optimized
- All templates: Mobile-responsive

- Format: PNG, JPG recommended

## Customization Tips

### For Professional (BKGE) Template
1. **Compliance Fields**: Automatically populated from customer data
2. **Payment Terms**: Edit the 4-row table in quotation settings
3. **Header Color**: Currently teal (#0f766e) - can be customized
4. **Signature Lines**: Left for Company, Right for Customer

### For Clean & Simple (AS) Template
1. **Keep It Simple**: Don't add too many compliance fields
2. **Logo**: Ensure it's square and 50x50px or smaller
3. **Signature**: Single authorized person recommended

### For Detailed Terms (TC) Template
1. **Bank Details**: Pre-fill in company settings
2. **Declarations**: Add your legal/compliance text
3. **Multiple Signatories**: All 3 signature lines available

## Troubleshooting

### Logo Not Showing?
- Ensure company logo is uploaded in Company Settings
- Check that URL is HTTPS (required for PDFs)
- Logo must be smaller than 50x50px

### Text Overflow?
- Clean & Simple: Keep company details concise
- Professional: Payment terms will wrap as needed
- Detailed Terms: Design handles longer content

### Print Issues?
- Set browser print scale to 100%
- Use minimal margins in print settings
- All templates verified for A4 paper

### Signature Lines Missing?
- Professional (BKGE): Both signature lines should appear
- Check PDF was generated successfully
- Try downloading instead of print preview
