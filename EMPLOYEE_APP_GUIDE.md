# 📱 Employee Attendance App - Installation & Usage Guide

## 📥 **How to Install the App**

### **Step 1: Download APK**
- Get `EmployeeAttendance.apk` file from HR/IT department
- Save it to your phone's Downloads folder

### **Step 2: Enable Installation**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Security** (or **Privacy**)
2. Enable **"Install from Unknown Sources"** or **"Allow from this source"**
3. Some phones: **Settings** > **Apps** > **Special Access** > **Install Unknown Apps**

### **Step 3: Install App**
1. Open **File Manager** or **Downloads**
2. Find `EmployeeAttendance.apk`
3. Tap the file
4. Tap **"Install"**
5. Wait for installation to complete
6. Tap **"Open"** or find app in app drawer

## 🔐 **How to Login**

### **Step 1: Get Your Employee ID**
- Contact HR department for your Employee ID
- Format: Usually like `EMP001`, `EMP123`, etc.

### **Step 2: Login Process**
1. Open **Employee Attendance** app
2. Enter your **Employee ID**
3. Tap **"Login"**
4. Allow **Location** permission when asked
5. Allow **Camera** permission when asked

## ✅ **How to Mark Attendance**

### **Check-In Process:**
1. Open the app
2. Make sure **GPS is ON** and you're at office location
3. Tap **"Take Photo"** button
4. Take a clear photo of your face
5. Tap **"Check In"**
6. Wait for **"Check-in successful"** message

### **Check-Out Process:**
1. Open the app at end of work day
2. Tap **"Check Out"**
3. Wait for **"Check-out successful"** message

## 📍 **Location Requirements**

### **Important:**
- Must be within **100 meters** of office location
- GPS accuracy should be **less than 20 meters**
- If GPS is poor, go to **open area** and try again

### **If Location Not Working:**
1. Enable **High Accuracy GPS** in phone settings
2. Go outside or near window
3. Wait 30 seconds for GPS to improve
4. Try again

## 📷 **Photo Requirements**

### **For Check-In:**
- Take clear photo of your face
- Good lighting required
- Look directly at camera
- Remove sunglasses/mask if possible

### **If Photo Fails:**
- Ensure good lighting
- Clean camera lens
- Take photo again

## 📶 **Offline Mode**

### **No Internet? No Problem!**
- App works **offline**
- Attendance stored locally
- Syncs automatically when internet returns
- You'll see **"Stored Offline"** message

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

### **"Employee not found"**
- Check Employee ID spelling
- Contact HR to verify your ID
- Ensure you're registered in system

### **"GPS accuracy too low"**
- Go to open area
- Enable High Accuracy GPS
- Wait for better signal
- Try again

### **"Outside office location"**
- Ensure you're at correct office
- Check with IT about office coordinates
- Try moving closer to main building

### **"Camera not working"**
- Grant camera permission in settings
- Close other camera apps
- Restart phone if needed

### **"Network error"**
- Check WiFi/mobile data
- Try again after few minutes
- App will store offline if needed

### **App won't install**
- Enable "Unknown Sources" in settings
- Check phone storage space
- Download APK file again
